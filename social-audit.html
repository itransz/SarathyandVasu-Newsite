Social Stock Exchange

SSEs allow ethical investors to invest in businesses aligned with their values.

Our country had embraced this innovative model and already implemented it through NSE & BSE.

Benefits of SSE for NPO/NGO


The SSE will enable SOCIAL ENTERPRISES/NPO’S to raise funds from the public, enhancing their visibility, wide reach and fostering transparency in fund mobilisation and utilisation.

With introduction of concepts of social stock exchange. it helps fund projects that impact society.

The role of SSE

The SSE will provide much-needed capital to social enterprises/NPO that often struggle to access traditional funding sources.

The SSE will enhance transparency by requiring regular audits of social activities and impact reporting. This will build trust among investors and stakeholders.

The SSE will increase the visibility of social enterprises, attracting both retail and institutional investors interested in supporting socially impactful ventures.
